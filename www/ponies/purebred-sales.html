Purebred Gotland Sales List

Here are our success stories: bred, raised and trained right here at Kokovoko. All ponies, unless noted, are problem and vice free. Of course, you're welcome to have a vet exam done on any pony you're considering buying. All are barefoot, will have a current coggins on departure, and will be current on Fluvac EWT, hoof trimming and worming. Transportation is available.

Kokovoko Valentino

Shown here during his first adventure driving single! Valentino is a 2011 bay gelding, 12.2H. By Dante out of Kokovoko Halley. Has a very quiet, steady disposition. Nothing we've done with him so far has upset him at all. Seasoned traveler already, been to Equine Affaire in MA! Has been ridden and driven single and pairs, and even had no objection to the water hazard his first time out!

Cute doesn't even begin to cover it. This guy is an all-around gem. Needs a special child in his life!
